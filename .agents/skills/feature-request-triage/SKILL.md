# Feature Request Triage

Use this skill to turn requested features into prioritized opportunities.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the product objective that prioritization should serve.
2. Group requests by underlying user problem, not proposed solution.
3. Estimate impact, reach, effort, risk, and strategic alignment.
4. Identify high-risk assumptions and cheap validation steps.
5. Recommend top priorities with now/next/later framing.

## Output

| Theme | User Problem | Evidence | Impact | Effort | Risk | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Call out requests that are symptoms of usability, onboarding, performance, or reliability problems.
